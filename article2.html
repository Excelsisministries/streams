DEALING WITH EMOTIONAL INSECURITY.

One can wholeheartedly say that this thing called 'Emotional Insecurity' is one that has caused some of the surging breakdowns in the heart of men. Emotion can precisely be exposited as a person's internal state of being and involuntary physiological response to an object or a situation, based on a 'physical state'. Thus, emotional insecurity is tersely a state of imbalance or unevenness that is repleted in emotion.
Truthfully, God as our creator, induced in us emotion. But, we sometimes ignorantly expose it unrestrictedly to the place(s) where it is tampered with and on the other way round, some people invade it and tamper with it because of the power they exercise over us.
Nevertheless, conflicting or fighting in agitation to ensure a balanced emotion is a massive risk to take, as it will culminate more breakdowns in the heart and more emotional imbalance. So, it is necessary that we do not get oblivious of the fact that emotional imbalance attracts parasites like depression, suicide and other malaise act.

I believe as the scripture asserts that one of the proclaimed blessings of God to man after creation was 'THE POWER OF DOMINANCE' on earth. Sadly, we see the expression of these blessings restricted to other things aside from ourselves. In other words, we sometimes fail to express this controlling power to ourselves personally when affective scenarios like trauma, pain, anger, and even excitement occur. When our emotions are really tampered with or when our emotions are used wrongly, there are two intrusive questions we ought to ask ourselves. I infer these questions to be; SHOULD OUR EMOTIONS CONTROL US? Or SHOULD WE CONTROL OUR EMOTIONS?

It is amazing that the same authoritative stance we possess in controlling the affairs of the earth, can be used to control our emotions in affective occurrences, especially the ones that can possibly cause breakdowns in our hearts. In essence, whether our emotions are tampered with or not, they should not control us, thereby causing us to act in unfit manners. We should control them by the enabling power of God caused by his blessings to man.
