JOY IN DISGUISE.

As the above preamble look like an illusion to some people and a negative proposition to some other people, I believe that a particular set of people sees it as a reality. These people are not just ordinary people. They are the chosen ones.
Isn't it bizarre to say that, what the earth calls life, is death before God? And what the earth calls death is life before God?
Sometimes, we back the wrong horse and choose to become dogs when we are trained to be lions. This happens because of the enticement the world throws at us.
We let pain tear us apart because we fail to look within.

In spite of this, it is healthy that we deem what the scriptures say in Romans 8:6-7 "...For to be carnally minded is death: but to be spiritually minded is life and peace. Because the carnal mind is enmity against God: for it is not subject to the law of God, neither indeed can it be"
When we travail in pains and despair, it always appears to us like the world is totally against us. But, it's good news when the world is against us, because we ought to live beyond the seen. God wants us to live in the unseen! The book of Ecclesiastes put firmly a bitter but true statement- "... Sorrow is better than laughter, for by the sadness of face, the heart is made glad (Ecclesiastes 7:3).
God teaches us the efficacy of light when darkness approaches.
When you wallow in tears, there's a working of brokenness done by God in you. It leads your soul to chase God swiftly.
Most importantly, be assured that God's plan for you is not of evil, but of good.
So, the hard times are intentional. Do not curse them when they arrive.
