ABOUT HEALINGS.

Do you want to be healed in your body?
Do you want to be healed in your soul?
Do you want to be healed in your spirit?
You can be made whole.
And
This time, it will last.

It's amazing how that God seeks the well-being of our innermost parts. Really, I wonder at how He can't just overlook some things and like us say, "I think we should just leave it like that, it doesn't have to be perfect, as long as it affects nothing". Instead, God says "No, it can't be like that, It can be perfect," and he won't stop till it is as he has said; perfect.
Have you ever just thought about yourself, your life, your dreams, your family and all that is yours, and just laugh, a sincere laugh, one that accompanies disbelief, that laugh that signifies that things will not get better and that if it does, not everything? Have you ever thought of that particular problem that seems to be the 'toughest' and shrug your shoulders or verbally admit the 'impossibility' of its termination?
See, God has boldly stated (boldly because he only speaks truth) that he desires for us to be healthy and prosperous, not just bodily, but in our souls. I like to believe that he speaks of all that we comprise, Every Single Detail. Healing isn't just for the 'sick'. It is for those who desire freedom, emancipation, light, joy, boldness, safety, love, redemption; the list is not exclusive to these .God has promised and he won't repent of it(change his mind), to being healing to you. Believe. Have faith. Your father is still very much in business and he knows you by name. None will perish on his watch, including YOU!
Do you want to be healed? Your Father is listening.
